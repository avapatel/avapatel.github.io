Economics and Computer Science

Along with my Computer Science degree and Artificial Intelligence concentration, I have taken several Economics courses that shaped how I think about data, markets, decision making, and real-world problem solving.

I am especially interested in work that combines economics and technology, such as using software, data analysis, and machine learning to better understand business trends, financial information, consumer behavior, and investment-related decisions.

One of the reasons I enjoy computer science is because it can take ideas from economics and turn them into actual tools, models, and applications that people can use.

Economics Courses
